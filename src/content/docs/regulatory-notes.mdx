## FAA posture

Public-safety drone operations can run under Part 107 or public aircraft / COA pathways depending on structure and agency status.

- FAA Public Safety Toolkit: https://www.faa.gov/uas/public_safety_gov/public_safety_toolkit
- FAA public safety drone program guidance: https://www.faa.gov/uas/public_safety_gov/drone_program
- Part 107 waivers: https://www.faa.gov/uas/commercial_operators/part_107_waivers

## Medical delivery warning

FAA states Part 135 certification is the path for small drones carrying property of another for compensation beyond visual line of sight.

- FAA package delivery / Part 135: https://www.faa.gov/uas/advanced_operations/package_delivery_drone

That makes AED/blood/medical delivery a later-stage opportunity, not the first wedge.

## Arizona legal notes

Arizona law places county search and rescue responsibility with the sheriff.

- ARS §11-441: https://www.azleg.gov/ars/11/00441.htm

Arizona also restricts drone interference with law enforcement, firefighter, or emergency-services operations and preempts many local drone rules.

- ARS §13-3729: https://www.azleg.gov/ars/13/03729.htm

Privacy/surreptitious recording law is relevant to video/data policy.

- ARS §13-3019: https://www.azleg.gov/ars/13/03019.htm

## Policy needs

Any agency-facing program needs:

- FAA authorization records
- pilot qualification logs
- maintenance logs
- battery lifecycle records
- public safety drone use policy
- privacy policy
- data retention schedule
- evidence handling rules
- public records posture
- incident command authorization workflow
- insurance and indemnity review

## Liability risks

- missed/delayed rescue
- bad thermal interpretation
- airspace conflict
- crash/injury/property damage
- dropped payload
- public privacy complaint
- chain-of-custody problem
- cyber/data breach
- operating outside approved mission scope
