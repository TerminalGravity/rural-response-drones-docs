# Offer Model

## Positioning

**Rural Public Safety UAS Readiness**

A grant-funded implementation partner for small and rural agencies that need drone capability but lack time, staff, policy, training, or procurement depth.

## Offer ladder

### 1. Grant-Ready Drone SAR Program Package

**Price:** $15K–$25K

For agencies that need a fundable concept before buying equipment.

Deliverables:

- needs assessment
- mission profile
- grant source map
- agency-owned vs contractor-supported model
- equipment spec
- lifecycle budget
- policy/SOP outline
- FAA compliance checklist
- training plan
- 90-day pilot plan
- presentation-ready concept note

### 2. Implementation Package

**Price:** $50K–$150K depending on grant size and hardware.

Deliverables:

- procurement support
- drone setup
- operator training
- thermal SAR workflows
- maintenance system
- exercises/drills
- incident command integration
- reporting and grant compliance

### 3. Readiness Retainer

**Price:** $1.5K–$5K/month

Deliverables:

- monthly/quarterly training
- battery and readiness checks
- logs and compliance records
- software/firmware updates
- annual grant refresh
- after-action reports
- limited on-call support

### 4. Surge / Incident Support

**Price:** contract-based day rate or standby agreement.

Only under agency command, with insurance and pre-approved procedures.

## What not to sell first

- proprietary drone hardware
- generic fleet software
- autonomous dock network
- medical delivery
- consumer emergency subscriptions
- “we replace SAR” positioning

## Economics snapshot

Assumption: $15K setup + $2.5K/month retainer per agency.

| Agencies | First-year setup | Annual retainer | First-year revenue |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | $15K | $30K | $45K |
| 3 | $45K | $90K | $135K |
| 5 | $75K | $150K | $225K |
| 10 | $150K | $300K | $450K |

The business becomes interesting only after multiple agencies/districts or a repeatable grant + implementation package.
