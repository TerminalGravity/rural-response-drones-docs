A working docs site for evaluating whether Jack should pursue a drone-based rural search-and-rescue / emergency response venture around Lake Havasu, Mohave County, and similar rural recreation markets.

<div class="decision-box">
<strong>Current thesis:</strong> viable as a grant-funded public-safety drone enablement company; not viable as a private “drone ambulance” startup on day one.
</div>

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="metric-card"><strong>Best wedge</strong> SAR, fire, EMS, OHV, lake rescue, emergency management drone readiness.</div>
  <div class="metric-card"><strong>Buyer</strong> Sheriff, fire, EMS, emergency management, city/county, rural districts, tribes.</div>
  <div class="metric-card"><strong>Revenue</strong> Grant prep, setup, training, maintenance, readiness retainer, surge support.</div>
  <div class="metric-card"><strong>Avoid first</strong> BVLOS medical delivery, proprietary drone hardware, consumer emergency response.</div>
</div>

## What this site is for

- Capture research and planning in one place.
- Convert the idea into a concrete pilot proposal.
- Track viable grants, buyers, risks, and validation steps.
- Prepare a concept note for Mohave County / Lake Havasu partners.

## What this site is not

- Not a claim that a startup should buy drones immediately.
- Not a replacement for sheriff/fire/EMS authority.
- Not legal, FAA, insurance, or grant compliance advice.
