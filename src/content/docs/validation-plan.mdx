# Validation Plan

## Rule

Do not buy drones first.

First prove agency demand, procurement path, grant fit, and willingness to partner.

## Target conversations

Start with 10 conversations:

1. Mohave County Sheriff SAR
2. Lake Havasu SAR unit
3. Mohave County Emergency Management
4. Lake Havasu Fire
5. Lake Havasu Police / marine patrol
6. Bullhead City or Kingman public safety
7. rural fire district
8. Arizona State Parks OHV/lake grant contact
9. amateur radio emergency communications group
10. local foundation / utility giving contact

## Questions

- Do you already have drones?
- What hardware/software do you use?
- Who flies them?
- What missions do you use them on?
- What breaks or gets underused?
- Do you need grant help?
- Do you need SOP/policy/compliance help?
- Do you need training or recurrent exercises?
- Would you use an outside vendor under incident command?
- What insurance/contract requirements apply?
- What unfunded capability is on your wish list?
- Which grants have you used before?
- Would you review or sponsor a pilot proposal?

## Minimum greenlight

Proceed if we get:

- 2 agencies willing to review a concept note
- 1 agency willing to act as applicant/sponsor/partner
- 1 specific unfunded need
- 1 matching grant cycle
- 1 written/email expression of interest
- clear procurement path for outside support

## First proof asset

Create a two-page concept note:

- problem
- solution
- pilot scope
- agency-owned capability model
- what the company provides
- equipment budget
- training plan
- grant targets
- measurement plan
- 90-day pilot schedule
