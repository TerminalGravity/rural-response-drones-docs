## Incumbents

### BRINC

Purpose-built public safety drones and LiveOps platform: 911 response drone positioning, thermal, zoom, 4G teleoperation, live streaming, evidence storage, CAD/911 integrations, and compliance support.

- https://brincdrones.com/
- https://brincdrones.com/responder/
- https://brincdrones.com/liveops/

### Skydio

US-made autonomous drones, public safety positioning, DFR, docked operation, Axon partnership.

- https://www.skydio.com/x10
- https://www.skydio.com/dock
- https://www.skydio.com/solutions/dfr

### Axon Air

Public safety bundle integrated with Axon ecosystem, Fusus livestreaming, evidence workflows, regulatory support.

- https://www.axon.com/products/axon-air

### DroneSense / Versaterm

Public safety drone operations platform for situational awareness, tactical operations, fleet management, and DFR workflows.

- https://www.dronesense.com/
- https://www.versaterm.com/solution/dronesense/

### DRONERESPONDERS

Major public-safety UAS nonprofit ecosystem and best-practice network.

- https://www.droneresponders.org/

## What not to compete on

- public-safety drone hardware
- generic fleet management SaaS
- CAD/evidence cloud against Axon/DroneSense
- full autonomous DFR network as first product
- cheap DJI-only pitch to public agencies

## Realistic whitespace

1. Local rural SAR drone operations support
2. Program-in-a-box for small agencies
3. Grant and procurement implementation
4. FAA/SOP/privacy/data-retention templates
5. Fleet migration and maintenance
6. Volunteer SAR training and exercises
7. Data products after incidents: maps, thermal annotations, after-action reports

## Strategic position

Be the local field layer, not the platform layer.

Help agencies make existing platforms useful, compliant, funded, and operational in rural terrain.
